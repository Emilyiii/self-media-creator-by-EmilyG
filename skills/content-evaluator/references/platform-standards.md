# Platform-Specific Standards

## 公众号 (WeChat Official Accounts)

### Format Requirements

**Structure:**
- Title: H1 (`# 标题`)
- Sections: H2 (`## 小标题`)
- Subsections: H3 (`### 子标题`)
- Body: Regular paragraphs

**Image Placement:**
- Cover image: After title
- Content images: Every 2-3 paragraphs or at section breaks
- Use `![描述](路径)` format

**Length Guidelines:**
- Minimum: 800 characters
- Ideal: 1500-2500 characters
- Maximum: 3000 characters (before reader drop-off)

### Quality Markers

**Good:**
- Clear value proposition in first 100 characters
- 3-5 main sections with H2 headers
- Concrete examples or data
- Clear conclusion with CTA

**Avoid:**
- Walls of text without breaks
- Missing introduction or conclusion
- Generic advice without examples
- No engagement prompts

### Engagement Elements

**Recommended:**
- Questions to readers
- Polls or interactive elements
- "留言告诉我" prompts
- Related article links
- QR codes for further engagement

## 小红书 (Xiaohongshu)

### Format Requirements

**Structure:**
- Hook line (first 2 lines visible in feed)
- Core insight (1-2 sentences)
- Key points (bullet list)
- Personal take
- Hashtags

**Visual Elements:**
- 3-5 emojis minimum
- Bullet points (• or -)
- Line breaks every 2-3 sentences

**Length Guidelines:**
- Minimum: 100 characters
- Ideal: 200-400 characters
- Maximum: 500 characters (hard limit)

### Quality Markers

**Good:**
- Attention-grabbing first line
- Clear value in first 50 characters
- Scannable bullet points
- Personal, authentic voice
- Relevant hashtags (3-5)

**Avoid:**
- Long paragraphs
- No emojis
- Generic content
- Missing hashtags
- Too promotional

### Engagement Elements

**Recommended:**
- Questions at the end
- "收藏备用" prompts
- "评论区见" engagement
- Save-worthy content (checklists, templates)
- Personal stories

### Hashtag Strategy

**Formula:**
1. 1 broad tag (#干货分享 #经验分享)
2. 2-3 niche tags (#AI工具 #效率神器)
3. 1-2 specific tags (#ChatGPT技巧)

**Total:** 4-6 hashtags

## Platform-Sensitive Content

### 公众号 Restrictions

**Avoid:**
- Direct competitor mentions (use "某工具" instead)
- Unverified medical/financial advice
- Excessive external links
- QR codes to competitors

**Use with Caution:**
- Political topics
- Sensitive social issues
- Copyrighted material

### 小红书 Restrictions

**Avoid:**
- Obvious ads without disclosure
- Competitor bashing
- Unrealistic claims
- Excessive self-promotion

**Use with Caution:**
- Before/after photos
- Price mentions
- External links (use "主页链接")

## Best Practices by Platform

### 公众号

1. **Hook in first paragraph**
   - Start with question, data, or story
   - Promise value clearly

2. **Use subheaders liberally**
   - Every 2-3 paragraphs
   - Make scannable

3. **Include visuals**
   - Minimum 3 images per article
   - Charts, diagrams, screenshots

4. **End with CTA**
   - Ask for comments
   - Suggest related reading
   - Invite follows

### 小红书

1. **Front-load value**
   - Most important info in first 2 lines
   - Assume 50% won't expand

2. **Make it scannable**
   - Bullet points over paragraphs
   - Emojis as visual anchors

3. **Be personal**
   - "I" not "we"
   - Share real experiences
   - Admit imperfections

4. **Engage immediately**
   - Respond to first comments quickly
   - Ask questions in post
   - Create save-worthy content

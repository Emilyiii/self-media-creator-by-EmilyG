# Content Evaluation Scoring Rubric

## Content Quality (30 points)

### Accuracy (10 points)

| Score | Criteria |
|-------|----------|
| 10 | All claims verifiable, sources cited, no factual errors |
| 7-9 | Minor unsupported claims, mostly accurate |
| 4-6 | Several unsupported claims or one major error |
| 0-3 | Multiple factual errors or misleading claims |

**Detection criteria:**
- Data/statistics without sources
- Absolute statements ("always", "never", "everyone")
- Unverifiable claims

### Depth (10 points)

| Score | Criteria |
|-------|----------|
| 10 | Deep analysis, multiple perspectives, nuanced view |
| 7-9 | Good analysis with some depth |
| 4-6 | Surface-level coverage, obvious points |
| 0-3 | Shallow, generic advice anyone could give |

**Detection criteria:**
- Word count (proxy)
- Specific examples vs. general statements
- Unique insights vs. common knowledge

### Originality (10 points)

| Score | Criteria |
|-------|----------|
| 10 | Fresh perspective, unique insights, personal experience |
| 7-9 | Some original elements mixed with standard advice |
| 4-6 | Mostly generic content found elsewhere |
| 0-3 | Complete clichés, no original thought |

**Detection criteria:**
- Cliché phrases
- Generic advice patterns
- Personal anecdotes

---

## Structure Quality (25 points)

### Logic (10 points)

| Score | Criteria |
|-------|----------|
| 10 | Arguments sound, evidence supports claims, no fallacies |
| 7-9 | Minor logical gaps |
| 4-6 | Some unsupported claims or weak connections |
| 0-3 | Major logical flaws or contradictions |

**Detection criteria:**
- Logical fallacies (strawman, false dichotomy, etc.)
- Missing evidence
- Non-sequiturs

### Coherence (8 points)

| Score | Criteria |
|-------|----------|
| 8 | Smooth flow, natural transitions, clear connections |
| 6-7 | Mostly coherent with minor jumps |
| 3-5 | Noticeable gaps between sections |
| 0-2 | Disjointed, hard to follow |

**Detection criteria:**
- Transition words usage
- Topic consistency within paragraphs
- Logical progression

### Completeness (7 points)

| Score | Criteria |
|-------|----------|
| 7 | Strong hook, clear thesis, satisfying conclusion |
| 5-6 | Minor issues with opening or closing |
| 2-4 | Weak hook or missing conclusion |
| 0-1 | No clear structure |

**Detection criteria:**
- Opening hook presence
- Conclusion markers
- Section balance

---

## Expression Quality (25 points)

### Readability (10 points)

| Score | Criteria |
|-------|----------|
| 10 | Perfect balance of sentence lengths, easy to read |
| 7-9 | Mostly readable with some long sentences |
| 4-6 | Frequent long/complex sentences |
| 0-3 | Hard to read, convoluted phrasing |

**Metrics:**
- Average sentence length (ideal: 15-25 characters)
- % of sentences > 50 characters (should be < 20%)
- Paragraph length variation

### Fluency (8 points)

| Score | Criteria |
|-------|----------|
| 8 | Natural flow, no awkward phrasing |
| 6-7 | Minor awkward spots |
| 3-5 | Several clunky sentences |
| 0-2 | Frequent awkward phrasing |

**Detection criteria:**
- Repetitive sentence structures
- Awkward word choices
- Choppy transitions

### Engagement (7 points)

| Score | Criteria |
|-------|----------|
| 7 | Compelling hook, storytelling, holds attention |
| 5-6 | Some engaging elements |
| 2-4 | Dry, textbook-like |
| 0-1 | Boring, hard to finish |

**Detection criteria:**
- Hook strength
- Story elements
- Rhetorical devices
- Active vs. passive voice ratio

---

## Platform Fit (20 points)

### Format (10 points)

| Platform | Criteria |
|----------|----------|
| **公众号** | Proper headers (##), image placeholders, section breaks |
| **小红书** | Mobile-friendly formatting, emoji usage, bullet points |

### Length (5 points)

| Platform | Ideal Range | Scoring |
|----------|-------------|---------|
| **公众号** | 800-3000字 | Full points in range, -1 per 200字 outside |
| **小红书** | 200-500字 | Full points in range, -2 per 100字 outside |

### Engagement (5 points)

| Platform | Criteria |
|----------|----------|
| **公众号** | Call-to-action, questions, comment prompts |
| **小红书** | Hashtags, questions, save-worthy content |

---

## Issue Severity Levels

### Error (Must Fix)
- Factual errors
- Platform violations (length, format)
- Logical contradictions
- Offensive content

### Warning (Should Fix)
- Missing sources
- Weak structure
- Poor readability
- Missing engagement elements

### Suggestion (Could Improve)
- Minor style issues
- Optional enhancements
- Alternative phrasing

---

## Score Interpretation

| Score | Rating | Action |
|-------|--------|--------|
| 95-100 | Excellent | Publish-ready |
| 90-94 | Good | Minor tweaks suggested |
| 80-89 | Acceptable | Address warnings |
| 70-79 | Needs Work | Fix errors and warnings |
| < 70 | Major Revision | Significant restructuring |

---

## Platform-Specific Thresholds

| Platform | Pass Threshold | Ideal Score |
|----------|---------------|-------------|
| 公众号 | 90 | 95+ |
| 小红书 | 85 | 90+ |

**Why different?**
- 公众号内容更深度，容错率稍高
- 小红书要求更紧凑、更抓眼球

## Source Evaluation Checklist

### Authority
- [ ] Author has relevant credentials
- [ ] Publisher has reputation
- [ ] Domain is established

### Accuracy
- [ ] Claims are verifiable
- [ ] Sources cited
- [ ] No obvious errors

### Objectivity
- [ ] Balanced perspective
- [ ] Discloses conflicts of interest
- [ ] Distinguishes fact from opinion

### Currency
- [ ] Publication date clear
- [ ] Updated if needed
- [ ] Within freshness window

### Coverage
- [ ] Sufficient depth
- [ ] Relevant to topic
- [ ] Appropriate scope

---

## Red Flags

**Avoid or verify carefully:**
- No author attribution
- No publication date
- Excessive typos/grammar errors
- Sensational headlines
- No sources cited for claims
- Domain registered recently
- Heavy advertising
- Known for misinformation
